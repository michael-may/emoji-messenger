Welcome!

This application can be used to send and receive encrypted messages.

It requires no internet connection and no personal information whatsoever.

Messages can be delivered with the protocol of your choice, including, but not limited to:

  • Email
  • Chat apps
  • MMS
  • Printed out pieces of paper

Messages are saved locally as customizable images with your text encoded directly into them; like this one below:

Don't worry, emoji are optional. Additionally, messages can be sent as QR codes for greater compatibility.

Some other features:

  • Password-protected keys/messages
  • Per-conversation encryption keys
  • Encrypted application data backup/restore
